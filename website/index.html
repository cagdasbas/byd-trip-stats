What it does

Everything your BYD knows — visualised

The app polls the vehicle SDK directly and turns raw CAN-bus telemetry into a rich, offline analytics suite that the factory infotainment never gave you.

Autonomous trip recording

Trips start and stop on their own from gear-position events (D/R → P). Brief stops stitch into one journey. Zero driver input, with manual override when you want it.

Real-time range projection

A consumption model fed from the BMS discharge counter — not the static factory estimate — self-calibrates over a rolling 10 km window and is bounded by WLTP.

Live motor & power telemetry

Per-axle motor RPM, power split, gear, speed, regen detection — plus HV/12 V bus voltage, tyre pressures and temperatures where the car exposes them.

Battery health & degradation

SoH over time with a least-squares trend line that projects the year your pack reaches the 80% warranty threshold. Cell voltage spread and thermal delta included.

Charging-session analysis

Full power / SoC / voltage / temperature curves while charging. Overnight and remote sessions are reconstructed from SoC deltas even when DiLink sleeps the app.

Deep charts & heatmaps

Custom Canvas charts for every metric, 14 correlation heatmaps with crosshair bin read-out, and a physics-based per-trip energy breakdown (drag, rolling, gradient, regen).

Offline route maps

OpenStreetMap route overlays with energy-event markers, rendered fully offline so your trips draw on the map without any connection.

Filter, sort & export

Multi-field filtering, six sort criteria, daily/weekly/monthly/annual consumption views, and per-trip export to CSV, JSON or a self-contained HTML viewer.

Optional integrations

Opt-in ABRP upload, outbound MQTT for Home Assistant, an offline-first PWA web companion, and encrypted Telegram or local backups. All disabled by default.

The range engine

Range you can actually trust

Most cars show you a single static estimate. BYD Trip Stats models your real Wh/km from the same BMS total-discharge counter that feeds the live consumption readout — then projects forward in three tiers.

  1. Live trip — a rolling 10 km window with EMA smoothing and a 3 km stabilisation period for the current drive.
  2. Historical speed bins — engages within ~0.2 km of moving, so the projection leaves the catalog baseline almost immediately.
  3. WLTP baseline — an upper bound that prevents implausible numbers during slow urban starts.

Every projection is compared continuously against the BMS estimate with a signed delta, so you always see how the model differs from the factory guess.

FAQ

Good to know

Do I need a subscription?

No. The app runs fully standalone on supported vehicles. Integrations like MQTT and ABRP are optional and disabled by default.

Is it really free, as I see premium features?

Yes, the app is completely free for personal use. Premium features are essentially for those users who wanted to show their gratitude for the work put into the project. Those premium features will remain as a "good thing to have" but will not be a part of the app's core functionality. Pro is a one-time €9.99 unlock (lifetime, per vehicle) — see the Pro section above for exactly what it includes.

How do I buy a Pro licence?

Install the free app, then open Settings → Preferences → BYD Trip Stats Pro and scan the on-screen QR with your phone (or copy your Vehicle ID) to email it to bydtripstats@gmail.com. You'll get a reply with payment instructions (€9.99, one-time) and a personal unlock code to paste in. The code is tied to your car, so it only unlocks that vehicle and survives app updates.

Can I use the app without side-loading?

Not currently. The long-term goal is for BYD to ship something like this natively. For now it's a side-loaded APK. If you want assistance with how to side-load, you can visit this page here (https://github.com/DeBondor/byd-unlocking-guide/blob/main/README_EN.md)

How do I install it?

Download the signed APK from GitHub Releases, enable installation from unknown sources on your DiLink unit, accept permissions by following the on-screen prompts, then pick your model on first launch.

Will it drain my 12 V battery?

Impact is minimal — the 12 V is continuously topped up from the high-voltage pack while the car is off. You can always set the app to minimal (reduced functionality) or off.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Everything stays on-device by default. The only outbound traffic is the opt-in Telegram, MQTT and ABRP integrations you configure yourself, plus a read-only update check.

Does it work on other BYD EVs besides the Seal?

If you car runs DiLink 3, regardless of being BEV or PHEV, then you can most definitely run the app.
